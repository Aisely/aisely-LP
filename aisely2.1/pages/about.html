About Aisely

We are an early-stage software startup. We are a small team, passionate about building software that help to get work done effortlessly and swiftly.
At Aisely, we are building the next big thing in the document automation space. In the world, there are over 217 million businesses out of which about 670,000 are large enterprises. In the U.S., there are 32.5 million SMBs and 21,000 large enterprises. In Nigeria, there are about 41.5 million enterprises, 99.8% of which are SMBs. And as large as this market is in these countries and in severa others across the globe, transactional documentation processes are still pretty much manual. For several billions of transactional activities that happen daily across the globe, professionals and business owners spend countless hours preparing and delivering transactional documents. It would take a professional a minimum of 10 minutes to prepare a simple transactinal document, while it could take up to an hour for a complex one.
We are building the biggest and most efficient solution for this space. We are building a means for businesses to create, share, sign, stamp, store and secure outstanding professional transactional documents painlessly, seamlessly and swiftly.
Would you like to know more? Feel free to contact us to know more about us and what we do.
